EA Sports FC Mobile

EA Sports FC Mobile is my favorite mobile game right now ! This is because FC Mobile features official teams, clubs, and most importantly, professional players. FC Mobile's gameplay and graphics are extremely realistic. Not only that, but the game uses enhanced animations and physics during games and matches. I can also build my Dream Squad (AKA Dream 11) that can consist of a variety of players.
